About Us

Jaipur Institute of Cricket (JIOC), established in 2017, is a premier sports academy focused on **developing young athletes**. Our expert coaches, top-notch training facilities, and **commitment to excellence** make us a top choice for aspiring players.

  • **RCB Net Bowler (2009)**
  • **KSCA Inter-Zonal U-16 Player**
  • **GDCA U-19 & Senior Division (MP)**
  • Passionate about **training young athletes**.
